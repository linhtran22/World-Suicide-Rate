Suicide is death caused by injuring oneself with the intent to die. A suicide attempt is when someone harms themselves with any intent to end their life, but they do not die as a result of their actions

The main risk factors for suicide are:

  • A history of suicide attempts
  • Depression, other mental disorders, or substance use disoder
  • Chronic pain
  • Family history of a mental disorder or substance use
  • Family history of suicide
  • Exposure to family violencem including physical or sexual abuse
  • Presence of guns or other firearms in the home
  • Having recently been released from prison or jail
  • Exposure, either directly or indirectly, to othersâ€™ suicidal behavior, such as that of family members, peers, or celebrities
